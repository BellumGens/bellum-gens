What is Esports Business League?

Esports Business League is a charity esports event, which provides working gamers with an arena for competition between individuals and teams from different companies. Each season of the league is 4-8 weeks in length. Matches take place on week days after working hours Monday-Thursday. The league culminates with finals where the teams and players who have accumulated the most points during the season meet. All league matches are being broadcasted live on our Twitch channels.

Terms and conditions

All team members must be current employees of the company associated with the team registration, should not have any active engagement or affiliation with a professional esports organization, and haven't participated in professional esports matches as players in the past 24 months from the date of registration. The team is allowed to have standins/substitute players and they have to comply with the same conditions as the main players on the team. Substitutes can be added to the team post registration, as long as they comply with the aforementioned conditions. Individual players (StarCraft II) must be current employees of the company associated with their registration. The company represented by the team/individual must be provided at the time of registration.

Registrations are open until the deadline is reached, {{ dates.regDeadline }}, or until all open slots have been filled (16 CS:GO teams, 32 StarCraft II players). Successful registration completion requires registration fee payment - 300 BGN (VAT incl.) per team or 60 BGN (VAT incl.) per individual competitor. Registration fee paymant is done with a bank transfer after the registration form has been submitted. All registration fees are being added to the prize pool fund. At the end of the season the prize pool will be donated to a charitable cause chosen by the winners!

Tournament format

CS:GO League

  • 3-7 weeks
  • 2 groups with up to 8 teams each
  • 1-2 matches per team per week
  • Matches are in BO1 or BO3 format
  • Round robin format
  • Each team earns points weekly
  • Finals weekend with the 4 best teams from the group stage
  • Full information about the format can be found here

StarCraft II League

  • 3-7 weeks
  • 4 groups with up to 8 players each
  • 1-2 matches per player per week
  • Matches are in BO3 format
  • Round robin format
  • Each player earns points weekly
  • Finals weekend with the 8 best players from the group stage
  • Full information about the format can be found here
