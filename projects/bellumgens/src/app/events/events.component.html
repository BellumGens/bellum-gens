The Venue

The Summer Theater in Stara Zagora is located at the foot of largest city park in Bulgaria. This open-air masterpiece combines nature and ancient traditions with modern architecture into a unique experience for the audience.

Stara Zagora

The city hosting this event has more than 8000 years of history! Ancient forums, Roman murals and mosaics, 2000-year-old theaters, bath houses, and aqueducts, it's all beneath your feet and everywhere around you. Traditions and modern esports come together at this unique place!
